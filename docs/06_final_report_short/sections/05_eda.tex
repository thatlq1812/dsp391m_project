\subsection{Key Findings}

Four critical insights inform STMGT design: (1) Multi-modal distribution (3 regimes) motivates GMM; (2) Strong temporal patterns justify Transformer; (3) Spatial correlation $\rho \approx 0.8$ (adjacent), decaying after 3 hops, validates 2--3 GNN layers; (4) Precipitation impact (15--30\% reduction) motivates weather cross-attention.
